Combination openFPGA core for Namco Galaga hardware, combining the Galaga, Dig Dug and Xevious cores.

Note: You must provide your own roms to use this core.

Analogue Pocket ports by Marcus Andrade (Boogermann). Original FPGA implementations are the work of Dar (Galaga/Xevious), MiSTer-X (Dig Dug).
